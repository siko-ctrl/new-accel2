What is a token?

A token is a digital unit of value that is created and managed on a blockchain. Tokens can represent anything from a physical asset to a digital asset, and can be used for a variety of purposes such as currency, voting rights, access to a service or product, or as an investment. They are often used in Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) as a way to raise capital, and can be traded on cryptocurrency exchanges.
